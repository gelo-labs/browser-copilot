GeloTools: Shorts Blocker

About

GeloTools: Shorts Blocker helps you regain control over your time by blocking addictive short-form content on popular social media platforms.

Statistics

Average time spent daily on short-form content:

  • TikTok: 95 minutes
  • Instagram Reels: 53 minutes
  • YouTube Shorts: 60 minutes

That's more than 5 hours a week, or almost 11 full days per year spent on auto-scrolling content.

Privacy

This extension is completely open-source and respects your privacy:

  • No data collection
  • No external connections
  • Minimal permissions required
  • Settings stored locally
